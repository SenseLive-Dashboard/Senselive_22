    About Us

    SenseLive is the platform to provide end-to-end IoT solutions for the industry to cope with the technological changes due to Industrial Revolution 4.0. We aim to convert the traditional industries into the smart industries by developing IoT architecture for more effective & efficient processes. We help to improve performance and reduce downtime through remote diagnostics, troubleshooting, condition monitoring capabilities, and predictive maintenance with the help of data management. Ultimately, helping to increase productivity and maintain standards.

    Our Mission

    To convert the traditional factory into smart factory by adding technology in the process.

    Our Services

    One-stop IoT solutions for end-to-end data management and complete analysis to manage operations inside the industry.

    Our Vision

    To bring out higher rate of productivity and improve efficiency of the industries.
